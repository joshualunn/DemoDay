About Me

I am a 17 year old African American youth. I am a resident of the Englewood area, and I have been all my life, many people have negative views of the area that I live in, however, my view on the Englewood area differs due to me actually being apart of the community. There are many positive aspects of the Englewood community that go unnoticed, due to the highlighted negative aspects. I feel that being part of Englewood Codes is a great way to display the positive that comes out of the community.
