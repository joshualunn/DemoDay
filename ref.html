About Englewood Codes

Englewood Codes is a great program that taught me many valuable things that will follow me through life. In the program we were introduced to coding and creating websites. We used HTML coding to create websites. We also used Cascading style sheets to style the websites. We also became familiar with other styling tools such as Twitter Bootstrap. We also learned and explored Using GitHub to host our handmade websites online. There was also a second aspect of Englewood Codes, which is the Leadership Courses. The leadership Courses teach participants about finding their true selves and recognizing their surroundings. I am very Grateful to be able to have Participated in this program. It helped me explore my creativity and pushed me to do deeper thinkig.
